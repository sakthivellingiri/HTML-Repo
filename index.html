Store Rainwater in Drums

Store rainwater in drums on your rooftop and in open areas around your home. Or you can build rain gutters on your roofs so that the rainwater can be collected in barrels or drums placed on the ground

Direct Rainwater Runoff to Plants

You can direct the rainwater runoff from your home structure to the base of plants. It can be used for plants that need regular watering and happen to be in the shade. rainwater for plants
